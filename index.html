MERI BHAINS KO ANDA KYUN MAARA !!

Objective: Simple enough. Bhains is throwing its sh*t at you. Dodge the sh*t and destroy the bhains.
Rules: Use arrow keys to move, spacebar to throw an egg. You can destroy the bhains by either colliding to it or hitting it with an egg.
